What this product is

This is an Ayurvedic, plant based cosmetic formulation that focuses on supporting dormant hair follicles and thinning hair, especially in women with widening hair parts. It reduces hair fall first, then supports visible regrowth over time.

Rooted in Ayurveda, guided by hair biology

The formulation works with the natural hair cycle, aiming to help follicles move from resting phase back into growth phase. It does not create new follicles, but is designed to reactivate existing ones that have become less active.

Observed clinical outcomes

  • Early visible changes in roughly 1 to 1.5 months of regular use
  • More significant improvements seen in the 3 to 6 month range
  • Continued use is required to maintain benefits

Cosmetic product. Individual results vary. Does not create new follicles.
